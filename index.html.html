About

I don't build single automations. I design the system around them.

A single Zap that moves a lead into a spreadsheet isn't an automation system — it's one step. The businesses I work with need the whole chain: capture, qualify, respond, book, notify, report, and recover when something breaks.

I plan the process before I touch a tool, then build it across n8n, Zapier, and GoHighLevel, wire in AI where it actually saves time, and connect the pieces with APIs and webhooks so nothing depends on someone remembering to do it manually.
